Hi! I'm Alexandra

Full Stack Web Developer

My full name is Alexandra Hionis. I am 28 years old and I live in West Chester, Pennsylvania. I am an aspiring full stack web developer and I am hoping to gain insight on what it takes to become a professional in the tech industry. I've earned a bachelor's in Psychology with a minor in Business from Temple University. It was during my education I realized my love for problem solving, being detail oriented, and pursuing a creative vision. Furthermore, the subdiscipline of psychology comes in effect with the field of interface design; an important faucet to consider as a developer.


As a full stack web developer and recent graduate of UPenn LPS Coding Bootcamp, I have built a toolbox of necessary technical skills, knowledge and a passion for the craft. These include HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, jQuery, Bootstrap, FireBase, Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, Express, Handlebars, and React. My fine attention to detail and creative expression allows for me to consistently deliver positive results and solve complex problems for any company, client, or team.


In my spare time I enjoy spending my free time with my girlfriend, family and friends, cooking, kickboxing and traveling.
